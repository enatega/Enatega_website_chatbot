# Enatega Milk Delivery Solution

## Overview
Enatega is a versatile and scalable solution designed to support online milk delivery businesses, whether for a single store or a marketplace of vendors.

## A Complete Milk Delivery Experience
The milk industry is evolving, and customers now expect more than just a checkout page. They seek a smooth, personalized, and reliable experience from browsing to delivery.

### Key Features
- **Flexible Delivery Options**: Customers can choose urgent or scheduled deliveries with ease.
- **Customizable Online System**: Tailor the milk delivery system to meet specific needs.
- **Real-Time Order Tracking**: Keep customers informed about their orders.
- **Delivery Scheduling**: Options for customers to plan their deliveries.
- **Personalized Features**: Include milk notes and tipping options.

## On-Demand Milk Delivery
Enatega offers:
- A wide selection of milk.
- Immediate or scheduled delivery options.
- In-app order personalization.
- Real-time updates throughout the delivery process.

## What You Get with Enatega’s Milk Delivery Platform
Enatega provides a comprehensive toolkit for delivery success, simplifying operational needs through various applications.

### Customer App
An intuitive milk ordering experience featuring:
- A clean product browsing and checkout interface.
- Real-time delivery tracking.
- Options for tipping, discounts, and feedback.
- Personalized milk note capabilities.

### Store App
Designed for store owners and partners to manage daily operations, including:
- Accepting and processing new orders.
- Updating product availability.
- Monitoring delivery progress.
- Accessing store-level analytics.

### Rider App
A delivery-focused mobile app that enhances team efficiency with:
- Instant order alerts.
- Map integration for accurate delivery.
- Access to order history.
- Real-time delivery updates.

### Admin Dashboard
A central hub for business management, featuring:
- Zone management and delivery scheduling.
- Order tracking and status updates.
- Store onboarding and vendor control.
- Reporting and performance insights.
