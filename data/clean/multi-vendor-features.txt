## Key Features
### Authentication
- **Email Verification**: This feature ensures the authenticity of user registrations, reducing the risk of fake orders and fostering a reliable customer base. It streamlines communication regarding order updates and delivery details.

- **Multi-Platform Authentication**: Simplifying the onboarding process, this feature allows customers to use their existing Google, Apple, or Facebook credentials, eliminating the need for separate account creation.
