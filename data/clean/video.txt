How to Setup Domain & DNS 
To set up your domain, review the details of what you are purchasing, including your domain name and order summary. Click on continue to proceed to the billing section. Fill in your billing details and click continue again to move to payment. Once the payment is completed, your domain will be successfully registered.

How to Setup Expo Account 
To create your Expo account, search for Expo and click on sign up. Enter your email, username, and password to create the account, then agree to the terms and complete the signup process. After logging in, go to the top-right corner and open User Settings. You may convert the account to an organization account if needed. This step is optional. You can either share your username and password or invite a team member to the organization.





How to Setup Firebase
Search for Firebase on Google and open the official website. If you are already logged into a Google account, you will be signed in automatically. Go to the console and create a new project by clicking “Get Started.” Enter a project name and accept the terms. You may enable Google Developer Program features, AI assistance, and Google Analytics if required. Set your location and complete any additional required details.
Once the project is ready, go to Project Overview and open Project Settings. You will find your project name and ID there. Navigate to Service Accounts and generate a private key. A JSON file will be downloaded. Share this file with the team.

How to Setup GitHub Account
Search for GitHub and open the official website. Click on sign up. You can continue with Google or Apple, or manually enter your email, username, and a password longer than 15 characters. Select your region and email preferences if desired. Complete the account verification process by solving the security puzzle. Once verified, your GitHub account will be ready.



How to Setup Gmail Account
Go to Google and select the option to create a new account. Enter your first and last name, choose your desired email address, and set a password. Confirm your password and proceed. Enter your phone number for verification, along with your date of birth and gender. Choose your personalization settings and accept the privacy and terms to complete the setup.

How to Setup Google Console Account
Search for Google Play Console and open the official link. Select your organization type. You will need a DUNS number, which can be obtained from the Dun & Bradstreet website.
Link a payment profile and pay the one-time $25 USD registration fee. Create a new payments profile and continue. Enter your DUNS number and provide the required organizational details such as company size and contact information. Complete the acknowledgments and finalize the registration.

How to Setup Amplitude Account
Search for Amplitude and open the official website. Click on Get Started and create a new account using your work email or Google. Select or create a new organization, provide its name and URL, and complete the setup. Once created, share your API key with the team.

How to Submit App Logos
You will need to submit your app icons. Refer to Apple and Google design guidelines to ensure your logos meet their recommended specifications. For iOS, follow the required dimensions, file type, color range, and size guidelines. Logos must be square. For Android, ensure the logo is not transparent and meets the specified dimensions and requirements.

Provide App Names
Provide names for the Customer App, Store App, and Rider App. Once finalized, submit all three names. These will be used to represent your apps on the App Store and Google Play.

How to Setup Apple Developer Account
Search for the Apple Developer Program and open the official website. To create an organizational account, you will need your company’s legal name, address, DUNS number, phone number, email, and website. The annual fee is $100 USD.
Sign in with your Apple ID or create one if necessary. Select your entity type and provide the required organization details. Review and confirm your information. Accept the agreements and complete the payment process. Your enrollment will then be processed.

Provide Color Scheme
Provide a color scheme consisting of three colors: primary, secondary, and accent. If unsure, you may use a color generator tool to select matching colors. Share the hex codes through the portal. If you have brand guidelines, you may share them via link or PDF.

How to Setup Netlify
Go to Netlify and click sign up. You can register using Google or your email. Enter your details, including name, company name, and team size. Once inside the dashboard, navigate to Usage and Billing. You may use the free plan or upgrade to a Personal or Pro plan if required.

How to Setup PayPal Account 
Search for PayPal and open the official website. Click sign up for free and enter your phone number for verification. Provide your email, name, and password to create your account. Enter your address and personal details, then agree to the terms. You may link a card immediately or skip this step for later. Once completed, your account will be ready to send and receive payments.

How to Setup Railway Account
Visit Railway and review the pricing plans. Select the Pro plan and sign in using GitHub or email. Accept the privacy and fair use policies. Enter your payment details and subscribe to the Pro plan. Your account will then be created.

How to Setup Sentry
Go to sentry.io and click Get Started. Create an account by entering your name, organization name, email, and password. Accept the terms and create your account. Skip onboarding if desired. Create a new project, select Node.js and Express as the framework, and name your project. Copy the DSN from the project dashboard and share it with the team.

How to Setup Stripe Account
Search for Stripe and open the official website. Click sign up and enter your email, full name, country, and password. Verify your email address. Activate payments by completing your business profile, including business location, type, and personal verification details.
Provide VAT details if applicable and select your industry. Add website or social media information if needed. Describe your order fulfillment process and provide public-facing details such as your store descriptor. Select your currency and enable two-factor authentication using an authenticator app, security key, or SMS. Save your backup code securely. Review your information and complete the setup. Your Stripe dashboard will then be active and ready to accept payments.

How to Setup Google Play Organization Account
To set up your Google Play Organization account, go to the Google Play Console by searching for it on Google and clicking the official link. Once there, select the type of organization you have. You will need a DUNS number, which can be obtained from the Dun & Bradstreet website by searching for it online and applying for a DUNS number.

On the next page, link a payment profile to your account. There is a one-time registration fee of $25 USD required to activate your Google Play Organization account. Create a new payment profile and click continue.

You will then be asked to enter your DUNS number, so make sure you have it ready. After entering the number, proceed by clicking continue and next.

Next, provide additional details about your organization, including its size, phone number, and other required information. Ensure all details are accurate before proceeding. You may be asked to provide further information and complete an acknowledgment. Fill in all required fields and continue to complete the setup process.


How to Setup MongoDB
Go to mongodb.com and click Get Started. Sign up using Google or your credentials. Accept the privacy policy and terms of service. You may personalize your setup or skip this step. Choose a cluster, such as the free tier for testing purposes, and create it. Once created, note your username and password and share them with the team.

How to Setup AWS Account and S3
Go to Amazon Web Services and create a new account. Enter your email address and account name, verify your email, and set a password. Choose a free or paid plan and provide your business and payment details. A small verification charge may apply. Confirm your identity using your mobile number and verification code.
Once your account is ready, access the AWS Management Console. Navigate to Services and select S3 under the Storage section. Create a new bucket by providing a bucket name and region. Configure the necessary settings and create the bucket. Share the bucket name and region with the team.
