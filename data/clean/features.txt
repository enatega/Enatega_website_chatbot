# Features of Enatega

## Customer Web/Mobile Application Features

### Email Verification
The email verification feature ensures the authenticity of user registrations, reducing the risk of fake orders and fostering a reliable customer base. This feature enhances the delivery information process, facilitating streamlined communication regarding order updates and delivery details.

### Authentication
Enatega provides multiple authentication methods, simplifying the onboarding process through multi-platform authentication. Customers can use their existing Google, Apple, or Facebook credentials, eliminating the need for separate account creation.

### Geolocation Restaurant Display
The geolocation restaurant display feature enhances user experience by showcasing restaurants on both the map and home screen, allowing users to easily discover nearby dining options.

### Comprehensive Restaurant Information
Users can access detailed restaurant information, including reviews, ratings, operational hours, delivery schedules, menu details, and minimum order requirements, enabling informed decision-making.

### Address Integration
The address integration feature simplifies adding and verifying addresses through integrated Maps and Google Places functionality. Users can easily add their addresses using mapping technology and suggestions from Google Places, optimizing the ordering process.

### Localization and Theming
This feature allows for personalization of the delivery app solution according to brand image. Users can select their preferred language and theme, creating a more engaging and user-friendly interface.

### Search Functionality
The robust search functionality empowers users to find specific restaurants or menu items, ensuring a smooth exploration experience within the custom web/mobile application.

### Push Notifications
Push notifications enhance customer engagement by providing constant updates about account creation, order status, and important announcements. Businesses can send global push notifications to keep all customers informed simultaneously.

### Real-Time Tracking
The real-time tracking feature allows customers to track the exact location and progress of their delivery, providing accurate and up-to-date information.

### Error Tracking
This feature improves system robustness by enabling real-time monitoring and reporting of errors. Integration with Sentry provides insights into errors as they occur.

### Performance Analytics
The performance analytics feature, powered by Amplitude, offers insights into user behavior, system responsiveness, and overall application efficiency, allowing for optimization of web app performance.

### Order Rating and Reviews
Customers can provide feedback through ratings and reviews after each order, offering valuable insights to improve the delivery business.

### Payment Integration
The payment integration feature supports both PayPal and Stripe, providing customers with secure and convenient payment options.

### Order History
The order history feature allows access to detailed insights into customer interactions, enabling business owners to track and analyze past orders for smarter decision-making.

### Favorites Management
Customers can easily mark and manage their favorite restaurants, enhancing loyalty and speeding up the ordering process.

### Food Customization
The food customization feature allows users to add variations of food items and include specific notes for restaurant preferences, ensuring a personalized touch to each order.

### Flexible Ordering
This feature meets diverse customer needs by offering pick-up and delivery options with varying timings, allowing users to choose what suits them best.

### Vouchers
The vouchers and promotions feature enables businesses to run promotions and loyalty campaigns, boosting customer engagement and building relationships.

### Tipping
The tipping feature allows customers to express appreciation for excellent service or delivery by adding tips during the checkout process.

## Restaurant Application Features

### Order Notification
The advanced order notification feature empowers restaurants to respond promptly to new orders, enhancing customer satisfaction through quick delivery.

### Time Limiter
The time limiter feature allows restaurants to control order processing by setting acceptance periods and customizing meal preparation times.

### Real-Time Order Receiving
This feature ensures seamless coordination as orders move through preparation and delivery stages, guaranteeing timely deliveries.

### Restaurant Status Updates
Partner restaurants can manage their online presence by switching their status between online and offline based on operational availability.

### Invoice Print Option
Restaurants can generate and print invoices directly from the Enatega delivery solution app.

### Delivered Orders History
The delivered orders history feature provides a comprehensive record of all delivered orders, including order date, items ordered, delivery status, and special instructions.

### Customer Order Details
This feature offers in-depth insights into current and past orders, including item lists, pricing, delivery status, and timestamps.

## Rider Application Features

### Real-Time Order Status Change
The rider application features provide instantaneous updates on delivery order status changes, ensuring timely information for business owners.

### Instant Dual-Alert System
Business owners receive instant notifications through push notifications and audible alerts as soon as an order is placed.

### Time Limiter
The time limiter feature allows business owners to set predefined time limits for different stages of the order fulfillment process, sending alerts for any delays.

### Address Mapping
Advanced mapping capabilities using Google Maps allow for precise mapping of delivery addresses, ensuring efficient navigation for the delivery team.

### Distance and Travel Time Display
This feature provides real-time insights into distance and travel time, calculating and displaying optimal routes for informed decision-making.

### Instant Customer Chat
Riders can address customer inquiries and provide updates instantly, enhancing the delivery experience.

### Order History and Order Progress
This feature offers a detailed record of previous orders, including order progress and delivery status, allowing for performance assessment.

### Online Delivery Fee Access
Business owners can set, modify, and optimize delivery charges tailored to their strategy.

### Quick Money Withdrawal
The quick money withdrawal feature allows for seamless and secure access to earnings.

### Wallet History
This feature provides insights into transactions, withdrawals, and customer payments, ensuring accuracy and transparency.

### Rider Status
Business owners can monitor the real-time status of their delivery fleet to ensure optimal performance.
